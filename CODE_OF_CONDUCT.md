# WellTrack Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
We are committed to creating a welcoming, safe, and respectful environment for everyone.  
WellTrack follows the principles of **non-discrimination** and **do no harm**.  
All contributors and users are expected to act in ways that support a positive community.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and considerate toward others.
- Use inclusive language.
- Provide constructive feedback.
- Accept different viewpoints and experiences.
- Follow project guidelines and instructions.

## Unacceptable Behavior
The following actions are not allowed under any circumstances:
- Harassment or discrimination of any kind  
  (including based on race, gender, age, nationality, language, disability, or belief)
- Personal attacks or abusive language
- Sharing harmful, offensive, or unsafe content
- Intentionally introducing bugs, malware, or security vulnerabilities

## Reporting Issues
If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please contact the project maintainer at:  
**i_sarsenbay@kbtu.kz**

All reports will be handled confidentially and respectfully.

## Enforcement
Violations may result in:
- A warning  
- Temporary or permanent removal from the project  
- Restriction from contributions or interactions

This Code of Conduct is inspired by the Contributor Covenant and adapted for the WellTrack project.
